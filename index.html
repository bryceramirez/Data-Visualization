HIV

in the Philippines

HIV stands for immunodeficiency virus.

HIV is considered to be one of the world's serious public health and social problems.

This might reduce the number of CD4 cells which the body unable to fight off infections and diseases such as cancer

AIDS is the most severe type of HIV infection which happens after the final stage of HIV.

As of September 2019, there are a total of 71,778 deaths due to HIV in the Philippines. A total of 3,617 have died.

In 2018, the Philippines has been considered as the country with the fastest growing HIV epidemic in the world by the United Nations Program. However, people still remain silent or are too shy to talk about it.
